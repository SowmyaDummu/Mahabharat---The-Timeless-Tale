Overview :

The Mahabharata is a Hindu epic that tells the story of a war between two groups of cousins over a kingdom. The Pandavas and the Kauravas fought for the throne of Hastinapur. The story explores themes of dharma, ethics, and spirituality...

Kurukshetra War :

The war between the Pandavas and the Kauravas for the throne of Hastinapura. The war is described in the Hindu epic poem Mahabharata...

KrishaArjuna

In choosing Lord Krishna as his advisor, Arjuna showed his loyalty and support in his friendship with Lord Krishna. Lord Krishna was not only Arjuna's friend, but he had guided Arjuna all the time. In the war of Mahabharata, the charioteer of Arjuna's chariot was also Lord Krishna himself...
